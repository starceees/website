About Me

Graduate Research Assistant passionate about autonomous systems and AI

Education

Currently pursuing graduate studies at New York University, working with the Agile Robotics and Perception Lab (ARPL) under Prof. Giuseppe Loianno. My research focuses on autonomous quadrotor systems, computer vision, and advanced control systems.

Research Focus

My work spans computer vision, deep learning, and signal processing for autonomous systems. I specialize in flight controller optimization, sensor integration, and real-time navigation systems for UAVs.

Research Interests

Computer Vision

Developing advanced algorithms for real-time object detection, tracking, and scene understanding in autonomous systems.

Deep Learning

Applying neural networks to solve complex problems in robotics, signal processing, and autonomous navigation.

Autonomous Systems

Research on quadrotor systems, flight controller optimization, and multi-agent coordination for complex missions.
